Travel and Accommodation

EasyJet and BA fly from London Gatwick to Bari and Brindisi. Ryanair also fly from Stansted to Bari daily.

There is a taxi service available outside of both airports, however this option can be expensive e.g. approximately €90 from Bari one way. Therefore we suggest trying to share this journey with a group of people where possible.

If there is enough interest and guests are arriving and leaving on similar timed flights, it is a possibility that a dedicated return transfer can be arranged to and from Noci.

Please could you let us know by 15th July if you are interested in this.

If you are planning to stay longer in Puglia and wish to explore the region further, we strongly recommend hiring a car.

For hotels and accommodation, we have agreed reduced rates at 2 lovely hotels and 4 small self contained apartments.

  • Santa Rosa Relais
  • Dimora Intini
  • Suite San Pietro

All accommodation is located in the Old Town of Noci and close to local amenities and importantly walking distance from the wedding venue.

Prices range from €50-€130 per room per night from Single up to Quadruple size and all include breakfast. There are 19 rooms available, which can accommodate up to 50 guests.

These rates are available for 3 nights, from Friday 30 September to Monday 3 October inclusive. To book, please contact Mr Marco Amatulli directly at Santa Rosa quoting Cross Wedding who will coordinate the booking.

It would be helpful if you can let us know if you are not planning to use these accommodation options so we can keep track of numbers.
