Travel and Accommodation

EasyJet and BA fly from London Gatwick to Bari and Brindisi. Ryanair also fly from Stansted to Bari

There is a taxi service available outside of both airports, however this option can be expensive e.g. approximately 90 Euros from Bari one way. Therefore we suggest trying to share this journey with a group of people where possible.

Alternatively it is possible to arrange a dedicated return transfer through our planners, depending on flight arrival/departure times. If you are interested in this, please email betheug@gmail.com by 30 August and we will enquire.

If you are planning to stay longer in Puglia and wish to explore the region further, we strongly recommend hiring a car.
