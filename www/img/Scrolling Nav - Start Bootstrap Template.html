Getting Here and Accommodation

Travel

EasyJet and BA fly from London Gatwick to Bari and Brindisi
There is a taxi service available outside of both airports, however this option can be expensive e.g. approximately 90 Euros from Bari one way - therefore we suggest trying to share this journey with a group of people where possible. Alternatively it might be possible to arrange a dedicated return transfer through our planners, depending on flight arrival/departure times. If you are interested in this, please email betheug@gmail.com by 30 August
If you are planning to stay longer in Puglia and wish to explore the region further, we strongly recommend hiring a car
